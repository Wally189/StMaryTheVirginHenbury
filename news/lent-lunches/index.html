Lent lunches return each Friday in the church hall

Lent lunches run every Friday from 12.15pm to 1.30pm in the hall.

Soup, bread, tea, and coffee are provided. Donations are invited, and this year all gifts go to the parish hardship support fund.

If you are new, please just turn up. A church steward will greet you at the hall door.
