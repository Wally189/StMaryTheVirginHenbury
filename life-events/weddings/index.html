Parish information

Weddings

A church wedding offers prayer, commitment, and celebration in a setting rooted in parish life.

Practical steps

What happens next

  1. Contact the parish office with your preferred date.
  2. We will confirm eligibility under Church of England rules.
  3. You will meet the clergy to plan the service and prepare together.

We can also advise on wedding blessings and thanksgiving services.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Can we marry at St Mary's if we live elsewhere?
This may be possible if you have a qualifying connection with the parish.
How much does a wedding cost?
Statutory Church of England fees apply, with optional extras such as bells or organ.
What are banns?
Banns are a public legal announcement of your intention to marry, read in church before the wedding.
