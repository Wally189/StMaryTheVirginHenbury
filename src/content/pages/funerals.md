---
title: Funerals
intro: We are here to support families in grief with compassionate, practical guidance.
nextSteps:
  - Ask your funeral director to contact St Mary's, or contact us directly.
  - The clergy team will speak with you to plan prayers, readings, and music.
  - We will confirm the order of service and support you on the day.
faqs:
  - question: Can funerals be held at church and crematorium?
    answer: Yes. We regularly support services in church, at crematorium chapels, or at graveside.
  - question: Is ongoing support available after the funeral?
    answer: Yes. We can offer pastoral visits and details of local bereavement support.
  - question: Can we include personal tributes?
    answer: Yes, and we can help you place tributes in the service appropriately.
---
If someone is near end of life, please contact us for prayer and pastoral ministry.
