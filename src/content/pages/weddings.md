---
title: Weddings
intro: A church wedding offers prayer, commitment, and celebration in a setting rooted in parish life.
nextSteps:
  - Contact the parish office with your preferred date.
  - We will confirm eligibility under Church of England rules.
  - You will meet the clergy to plan the service and prepare together.
faqs:
  - question: Can we marry at St Mary's if we live elsewhere?
    answer: This may be possible if you have a qualifying connection with the parish.
  - question: How much does a wedding cost?
    answer: Statutory Church of England fees apply, with optional extras such as bells or organ.
  - question: What are banns?
    answer: Banns are a public legal announcement of your intention to marry, read in church before the wedding.
---
We can also advise on wedding blessings and thanksgiving services.
