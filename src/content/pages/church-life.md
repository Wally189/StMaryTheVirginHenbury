  - title: Exploring faith
    text: Regular short courses for people asking questions about Christian faith.
  - title: Volunteering and rotas
    text: Join welcome, reading, intercessions, serving, refreshments, and practical churchyard teams.
  - title: Giving
    text: Planned giving, one-off donations, and practical support for parish ministry and building care.
