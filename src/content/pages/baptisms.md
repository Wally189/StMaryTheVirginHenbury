---
title: Baptisms
intro: Baptism marks the beginning of a journey in Christian faith and belongs to people of all ages.
nextSteps:
  - Send an enquiry to the parish office using the form below.
  - A member of clergy will contact you to arrange a conversation.
  - We will agree a service date and explain practical details for the day.
faqs:
  - question: Do we have to be regular churchgoers?
    answer: No. We ask families to attend church before the service so we can get to know one another.
  - question: Is there a fee for baptism?
    answer: There is no fee for baptism itself.
  - question: Can adults be baptised?
    answer: Yes. Adults can be baptised and confirmed after preparation with the clergy.
---
We hold baptism services during Sunday worship and at agreed afternoon services.
