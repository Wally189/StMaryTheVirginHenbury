---
title: New here
intro: If this is your first Sunday at St Mary's, this page explains what to expect and how to find your way.
sections:
  - title: What to expect on Sunday
    items:
      - Our 10.00am Parish Eucharist usually lasts about an hour.
      - You can sit anywhere. A welcomer will give you an order of service.
      - We sing hymns, listen to readings and a sermon, pray, and share Holy Communion.
  - title: Accessibility
    items:
      - Step-free access is available through the south porch.
      - Accessible toilet and baby changing are in the church hall.
      - Large-print service sheets are available on request.
  - title: Parking and transport
    items:
      - Limited parking is available near the churchyard entrance.
      - Street parking is available on nearby roads; please be considerate of neighbours.
      - First Bus routes serving Henbury stop within a short walk of church.
  - title: Families and children
    items:
      - Junior Church runs most term-time Sundays during the 10.00am service.
      - A small play corner is available in church for younger children.
      - Families are welcome to stay together in church if preferred.
  - title: Holy Communion
    items:
      - Confirmed communicant members of any Christian tradition are welcome to receive Communion.
      - If you would prefer a blessing, please come forward with arms crossed.
---
