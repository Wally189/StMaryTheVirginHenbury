Privacy notice

St Mary the Virgin, Henbury keeps personal data only for parish administration, pastoral care, and communication about church life.

Contact forms on this site send information to the parish office. We do not sell personal data. You can request access, correction, or deletion by emailing office@stmaryhenbury.org.uk.

For full parish data policy details, contact the Parish Administrator.
