Safeguarding

Safeguarding is part of our Christian responsibility. If you have a concern, please contact us immediately.

Urgent action

Urgent guidance

  • If someone is in immediate danger, call 999.
  • If you have concerns about a child or adult at risk, contact the Parish Safeguarding Officer as soon as possible.
  • If the concern involves someone in parish leadership, contact the Diocese of Bristol Safeguarding Team directly.
